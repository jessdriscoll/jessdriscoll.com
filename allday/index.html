All Day Breakfast is a tiny book-and-bakeshop. I am your baker and your teacher, Jess Driscoll. I'm a home baker, raised and taught in my mother's kitchen, but I'm also Food Safe certified. Each market, I'll have whole wheat sourdough loaves for sale, as well as sweet and savoury experiments. In addition to baked goods, All Day Breakfast sells stickers, buttons, and zines--the perfect something to read as you relax with toast and tea. For the curious, my sourdough starter zine is available as a PDF for you to download and print at home. You can find the booth on the first two Sundays of each month at the White Rock Farmers Market, but if you're not a local, you can shop provisions online.
