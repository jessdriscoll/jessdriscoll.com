All Day Breakfast is a tiny shop. During the summer, we sell fresh baked bread at the farmers market. During the winter, we write and publish books. You can buy everything in season online. To go with bread and books, ADB has local sea salt, made at home with water collected from Crescent Beach, and a traditional sourdough starter kit, containing dried starter. The instructions are included as a booklet, which is also available to read online and as a PDF to download and print at home.
