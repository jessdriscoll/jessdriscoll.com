This website is the the most recent iteration of a blog I started on Blogspot in 2002. You can call me Jess or Jessica; my pronouns are she/her and singular they. I teach kids how to read and write, and I write zines and bake sourdough bread for the farmers market. I live and work on the unceded territory of the Semiahmoo Nation. My commitment to unsettling Turtle Island includes redistributing 10% of my income to support Indigenous land defenders.
