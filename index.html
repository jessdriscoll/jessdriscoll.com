I write tiny stories and make tiny books. My books are available in print on Etsy and as digital downloads on Itch.io.

My work lives in the space between analogue and digital. This website is only the most recent version of a blog I started on blogspot.com in 2002. I teach workshops for kids and curious adults.

I’m a settler Canadian who lives on the unceded land of the Semiahmoo First Nation, neither British Columbia nor Washington. I was born on the last day of 1981, both Generation X and Millennial. My pronouns are she/her. You can call me Jess or Jessica.
