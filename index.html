Born on the last day of 1981, I was a Generation X teenager, and now I'm a Millennial adult. My pronouns are she/her. I go by Jess online and Jessica IRL. This website is only the most recent version of a blog I started with Blogger in 2002. I write tiny stories and make tiny books.

As a settler Canadian, I acknowledge the Semiahmoo First Nation and their unceded land where I live. The white rock which gave my city its name and tourist monument got its colour from bat guano. Now they repaint it every month because the bats are gone.
