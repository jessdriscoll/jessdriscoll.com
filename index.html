I'm a teacher who makes zines and a settler Canadian who lives on the unceded land of the Semiahmoo First Nation, neither British Columbia nor Washington. I was born in 1981, both Generation X and Millennial. This website is only the most recent version of a blog I started on blogspot.com in 2002.

My work comes from the spaces between analogue and digital. I host the Pen & Paper Club at my local library and teach zine-making workshops for kids and curious adults. Contact me to request a workshop for your group.
