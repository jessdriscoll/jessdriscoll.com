I write tiny stories and make tiny books. This website is only the most recent version of a blog I started on blogspot.com in 2002. My books are available in print and digital. I teach workshops for kids and curious adults.

I’m a settler Canadian who lives on the unceded land of the Semiahmoo First Nation, neither British Columbia nor Washington. I was born on the last day of 1981, both Generation X and Millennial. My pronouns are she/her. You can call me Jess or Jessica.
